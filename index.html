Our Comprehensive Tajweed Course

Course Overview

Our Tajweed course is designed specifically for Muslim women who want to perfect their Quranic recitation. With expert female instructors and a supportive community, you'll master the rules of Tajweed in a comfortable, women-only environment.

Course Structure

12-week comprehensive program
Weekly live online sessions (2 hours each)
Small group classes (maximum 10 students)
One-on-one feedback sessions
Access to recorded lessons
Private WhatsApp group for support

What You Will Learn

Correct pronunciation of all Arabic letters (Makharij Al-Huroof)

Rules of Noon Saakin and Tanween (Izhaar, Idghaam, Iqlaab, Ikhfaa)

Rules of Meem Saakin (Ikhfaa Shafawi, Idghaam Shafawi, Izhaar Shafawi)

Different types of Madd (elongation) and their proper duration

Rules of stopping (Waqf) and starting (Ibtidaa)

Special letters and their unique characteristics (Sifaat Al-Huroof)

Common mistakes in recitation and how to avoid them

By the End of This Course

You will be able to recite the Quran with proper Tajweed rules

You will recognize and correct mistakes in your own recitation

You will understand the reasoning behind each Tajweed rule

You will have memorized selected surahs with perfect pronunciation

You will receive a certificate of completion

You will join our community of graduates with ongoing support

You will have the confidence to teach basic Tajweed to others

About Us

We are dedicated to providing free, high-quality Tajweed education to Muslim women worldwide. Our mission is to empower women to connect with the Quran through proper recitation.

Founded in 2020, our community has grown to include hundreds of students from over 50 countries, all learning in a supportive, women-only environment.

Our Vision

To create a global community of Muslim women proficient in Quranic recitation.

Our Values

Inclusivity, excellence, sisterhood, and dedication to authentic knowledge.
